MenB for young people

Book a MenB vaccination appointment

You can use this service if you were born between 1 September 2007 and 31 August 2008 and are registered with a GP surgery in England.

Find out how to register with a GP surgery if you are not registered.

Information:

The MenB vaccine is given in 2 doses, which need to be at least 4 weeks apart.

How it works

Use this service to:

  • book an appointment for your 1st or 2nd dose
  • check your existing appointment details
  • change an appointment
  • cancel an appointment

Older students

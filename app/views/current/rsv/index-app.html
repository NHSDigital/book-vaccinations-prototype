Book, change or cancel an RSV vaccination appointment

Use this service to:

  • book a vaccination appointment at a pharmacy
  • check your existing appointment details
  • change your appointment
  • cancel your appointment

Important

Currently, you can only book online appointments at some GP surgeries and at pharmacies in some areas of Essex, Lancashire, Leicestershire, London, Merseyside, Suffolk and the West Midlands.

You can currently use this service if either:

  • you’re aged 75 and over
  • you’re pregnant (over 28 weeks)
  • you're a resident of a care home for older adults

More pharmacies will offer RSV vaccinations soon. If you cannot find an appointment in your area, contact your GP surgery.

Booking for 2 people

You can book appointments for 2 people on the same day.

How it works

The service will check if you need a vaccine before you book.

Start now

Find a walk-in centre

You can find a walk-in RSV vaccination site that offers walk-in appointments without booking.

Book over the phone

You can call 119 free of charge to book over the phone if you cannot book online. You can speak to a translator if you need to.

If you have difficulties communicating or hearing

You can use textphone 18001 or the NHS 119 British Sign Language (BSL) interpreter service if you have difficulties communicating or hearing, or if you are a BSL user.

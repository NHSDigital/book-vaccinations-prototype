Book, cancel or change a COVID-19 vaccination appointment

Use this service to:

  • check if you need an appointment
  • book an appointment
  • check your exisiting appointment details
  • cancel your appointment

You can use this service if you are registered with a GP surgery in England. Find out how to register with a GP surgery if you're not registered.

Important: Online COVID-19 bookings closed

You can no longer book a COVID-19 vaccination using this service. this also applies to the 119 service.

The service will reopen later in the year. If your NHS record suggests you may be eligible for a further COVID-19 vaccination, the NHS will contact you.

Are you able to get a free flu vaccine?

as you're under 65, you're only able to get a free flu vaccine if you:

  • have a serious long-term health condition
  • are pregnant
  • are in long-stay residential care
  • recieve a carer's allowance, or are the main carer for an older or disabled person who may be at risk if you get sick
  • live with someone who is more likely to get infections (such as someone who has HIV, has had a transplant or is having certain treatments for cancer, lupus or rheumatoid arthritis)
  • a frontline health or social care worker (if your wokrplace doesn't offer an occupational health scheme)

You might be asked some questions at the pharmacy to check your eligible for a free flu vaccine.

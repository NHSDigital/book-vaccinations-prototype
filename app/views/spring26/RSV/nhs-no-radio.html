Do you know your NHS number?

An NHS number is a 10 digit number, like 485 777 3456.

You can find it:

  • on any letter the NHS has sent someone
  • on a prescription
  • by logging in to a GP practice online service
  • by using the find your NHS number service (link opens in a new tab)

Book or cancel an RSV (respiratory syncytial virus) vaccination at a pharmacy

Use this service to:

  • book a vaccination appointment at a pharmacy
  • check your existing appointment details
  • change your appointment
  • cancel your appointment

Currently you can only book appointments at pharmacies in parts of the East of England.

Getting a vaccination at a GP surgery

You can also get an RSV vaccination at a GP surgery. You will need to wait to be invited by your GP if you are eligible.

How it works

The service will check if you need a vaccine before you book.

You'll need the booking reference number we sent you to change or cancel your appointment.

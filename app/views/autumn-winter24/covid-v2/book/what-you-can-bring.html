Things you can bring on the day to show that you need a vaccine

It can help to bring one of these things on the day:

  • a hospital letter that describes the condition or treatment causing you to have a weakened immune system
  • a prescription or a medicine box with your name and date showing when the medicine was prescribed - this must be for the condition or treatment causing you to have a weakened immune system.
  • a letter from a GP or hopsital specialist asking you to get a vaccine for people with a weakened immune system

If you do not have a suitable letter, prescription or medicine box, you may still be able to recieve your vaccination by talking to someone at the appointment.

Book, change or cancel an RSV vaccination at a pharmacy

Use this service to:

  • book a vaccination appointment at a pharmacy
  • check your existing appointment details
  • change your appointment
  • cancel your appointment

Important

Currently, you can only book online appointments at pharmacies in some areas of Essex, Leicestershire, Suffolk, the West Midlands, London and the North West.

You can currently use this service if:

  • you’re aged 75-79 years old
  • you’re pregnant (over 28 weeks)

More pharmacies will be offering RSV vaccinations soon. If you cannot find an appointment in your area, please contact your GP surgery.

How it works

The service will check if you need a vaccine before you book.

You'll need the booking reference number we sent you to change or cancel your appointment.

Getting an appointment at a GP surgery

You can also get an RSV vaccination at a GP surgery.

Your GP surgery may contact you about getting the RSV vaccine. This may be by letter, text, phone call or email. You do not need to wait to be contacted before booking your vaccination.

If you're over 80, you can only get vaccinated at your GP surgery. You cannot book online.

Book over the phone

You can call 119 free of charge to book over the phone if you cannot book online.

You can speak to a translator if you need to.

Find a pharmacy walk-in service

You can find a walk-in RSV vaccination site to get a vaccination without an appointment. You do not need to be registered with a GP.

Book, cancel or change a free NHS flu vaccination appointment at a pharmacy

Use this service to:

  • check if you need an appointment
  • book an appointment
  • check your exisiting appointment details
  • change an appointment
  • cancel an appointment

COVID-19 vaccination

You may also be offered a COVID-19 vaccination in the same appointment, if you are eligible.

How it works

The service will check if you need a vaccination before you book.

To change or cancel an appointment, you'll need the booking reference number we sent you.

Book a vaccination appointment
View, cancel or change an existing appointment

Book over the phone

You can call 119 free of charge to book over the phone if you cannot book online.

You can speak to a translator if you need to.

If you have difficulties communicating or hearing

You can use textphone 18001 or the NHS 119 British Sign Language (BSL) interpreter service if you have difficulties communicating or hearing, or if you are a BSL user.

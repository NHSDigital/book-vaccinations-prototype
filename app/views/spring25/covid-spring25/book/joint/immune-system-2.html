Are they able to get a COVID-19 vaccination?

As you're under 75 you're only able to get a COVID-19 vaccine if you:

  • have a weakened immune system because of a health condition or treatment
  • live in a long-stay residential care home for older adults

You might be asked some questions at the pharmacy to check you're eligible for a COVID-19 vaccination.

If you are not sure, you can read more about who is eligible for a COVID-19 vaccine

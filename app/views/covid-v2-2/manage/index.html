Use this service to:

Important: Online COVID-19 bookings closed

You can no longer book a COVID-19 vaccination using this service. this also applies to the 119 service.

The service will reopen later in the year. If your NHS record suggests you may be eligible for a further COVID-19 vaccination, the NHS will contact you.

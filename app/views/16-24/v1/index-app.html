Book a 16-24 vaccine catch-up appointment

You can use this service if you've been invited for one of these vaccines:

  • MMR
  • HPV
  • MenACWY
  • Td/IPV

If you need more than one of these, you can get them at the same time. You only need to book one appointment

Who can use this service

You can use this service if you are 16-24 and are registered with a GP surgery in England.

Find out how to register with a GP surgery if you are not registered.

Book over the phone

You can call 119 free of charge to book over the phone if you cannot book online. You can speak to a translator if you need to.

If you have difficulties communicating or hearing

You can use textphone 18001 or the NHS 119 British Sign Language (BSL) interpreter service if you have difficulties communicating or hearing, or if you are a BSL user.

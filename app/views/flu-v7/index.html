Book, change or cancel a free NHS flu vaccination at a pharmacy

Use this service to:

  • check if you need an appointment
  • book an appointment
  • check your existing appointment details
  • change an appointment
  • cancel an appointment

You can use this service if you are registered with a GP surgery in England. You can find UK wide vaccination sites in Wales, Northern Ireland and Scotland.

Find out how to register with a GP surgery if you are not registered.

Information:

This service may also ask if you want to book a COVID-19 vaccine at the same appointment. COVID-19 vaccine eligibility has changed so please check you are still eligible before booking.

Booking for 2 people

You can book appointments for 2 people on the same day

How it works

The service will check if you need a vaccine before you book.

To change or cancel an appointment, you'll need the booking reference number we sent you.

Book over the phone

You can call 119 free of charge to book over the phone if you cannot book online.

You can speak to a translator if you need to.

Find a pharmacy

You can find a pharmacy that offers walk-in appointments without booking.

If you have difficulties communicating or hearing

You can use textphone 18001 or the NHS 119 British Sign Language (BSL) interpreter service if you have difficulties communicating or hearing, or if you are a BSL user.

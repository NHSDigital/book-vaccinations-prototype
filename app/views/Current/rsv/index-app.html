Book, change or cancel an RSV (respiratory syncytial virus) vaccination at a pharmacy

Use this service to:

  • book a vaccination appointment at a pharmacy
  • check your existing appointment details
  • change your appointment
  • cancel your appointment
Information:

You may also be offered a COVID-19 vaccine in the same appointment, if you are eligible.

Important

Currently, you can only book online appointments at pharmacies in some areas of Essex, Leicestershire, Suffolk, the West Midlands and the North West.

You can currently use this service if either:

  • you’re aged 75-79
  • you’re pregnant (over 28 weeks)

More sites will offer RSV vaccinations soon. If you cannot find an appointment in your area, please contact your GP surgery.

Booking for 2 people

You can book appointments for 2 people on the same day.

How it works

The service will check if you need a vaccine before you book.

Start now

Getting an appointment at a GP surgery

You can also get an RSV vaccination at a GP surgery.

Your GP surgery may contact you about getting the RSV vaccine. This may be by letter, text, phone call or email. You do not need to wait to be contacted before booking your vaccination.

Book, change or cancel an RSV vaccination

Use this service to:

  • book a vaccination appointment
  • check your existing appointment details
  • change your appointment
  • cancel your appointment
Information:

You may also be offered a COVID-19 vaccine in the same appointment, if you are eligible.

Important

Currently, you can only book online appointments at some GP surgeries and at pharmacies in some areas of Essex, Lancashire, Leicestershire, London, Merseyside, Suffolk, the North West and the West Midlands.

You can currently use this service if either:

  • you’re aged 75 or over
  • you’re pregnant (over 28 weeks)
  • you're a resident of a care home for older adults

More pharmacies will offer RSV vaccinations soon. If you cannot find an appointment in your area, contact your GP surgery.

Booking for 2 people

You can book appointments for 2 people on the same day.

How it works

The service will check if you need a vaccine before you book.

You'll need the booking reference number we sent you to change or cancel your appointment.

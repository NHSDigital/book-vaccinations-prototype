You can book a free NHS respiratory syncytial virus (RSV) vaccine because of your age. Find a pharmacy offering RSV vaccines at www.nhs.uk/find-rsv or contact your GP surgery. RSV affects the airways and lungs and can make it dififcult to breathe. It can lead to pneumonia and other life-threatening conditions. Your age group has a higher risk of getting seriously ill. Just 1 dose gives most people good protection for a few years. If you are 80, your last chnace to get the RSV vaccine is 31 August 2025. Read more: www.nhs.uk/rsv-vaccine.

Book or cancel an RSV (respiratory syncytial virus) vaccination at a pharmacy

Use this service to:

  • book a vaccination appointment at a pharmacy
  • check your existing appointment details
  • change your appointment
  • cancel your appointment

Important

Currently, you can only book appointments at pharmacies if you are living in or near to Suffolk and North East Essex and Mid and South Essex.

You can use this service if:

  • you’re aged 75-79
  • you turned 80 on or after 1 September 2024 (you’re eligible for the free RSV vaccine until 25 August 2025)

If you are pregnant, please get in touch with your GP or midwife for more information on getting the RSV vaccination.

Information:

More sites will offer RSV vaccinations soon. If you cannot find an appointment in your area, please contact your GP surgery.

Booking for 2 people

You can book an appointment for up to 2 people who are both over 18 years of age.

How it works

The service will check if you need a vaccine before you book.

You'll need the booking reference number we sent you to change or cancel your appointment.
